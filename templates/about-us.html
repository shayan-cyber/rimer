Artist Bio

Hello,
Itâ€™s Rimer

Currently based in Guwahati, Assam. I'm a passionate musician/writer. I've been working on music since the past two years and on more than one occasions, I confess, without exaggeration, it has saved my life.

I began writing because I didn't know how else I could talk about some otherwise shocking things. My music often deals with themes of generational hatred, suicide, mental illnesses, societal atrocities, class difference and (on brighter days,) self worship & love. Most of stuff is born out of a rather unhappy place in me and paradoxically enough, from a place of extreme self belief . I aspire to write for those who can't. I want to be the voice of those whose voices are unheard.

My influences vary from extreme subgenres of metal to poppy commercial music. My intentions vary from saving lives to showcasing glimpses of the afterlife. Currently flicking the pen to form rap verses. The future holds a lot more.

This website is just an attempt to preserve my works outside of social media.
